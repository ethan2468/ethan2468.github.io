Virtual Boy
 
 
The Virtual Boy
1995
 
The Virtual Boy was Nintendo’s first flop of a console. It was the very first take on virtual reality, but at the time it wasn’t seen as very interesting and didn’t have very many games nor did it look sleek compared to other Nintendo consoles. Not to mention, it was sold for $179.99 USD, which was not seen as a good price for what you were getting. Worldwide, it sold 770,000 consoles, only 140,000 of them sold in Japan which is obviously its native country. To add on top of it, the only color used in all the games was red and the 3D effect was quite bad and even caused people nausea. In less than a year, The Virtual Boy was taken off store shelves.
