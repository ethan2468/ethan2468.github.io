The Nintendo Switch
2017
 
At the moment, this is Nintendo’s newest console. But the Switch is what the Wii U could’ve been. The Nintendo Switch was hinted at years before its release, its original name being the ‘Nintendo NX,’ but then becoming the Switch and releasing in 2017 for $299.99. But this console is worth the price tag. This console is both a portable handheld and a console in one. Rather than the Wii U, the handheld itself is the console and uses a stand to be transferred to the TV screen. So far in the 2 and a half years it has been out, the Switch has had some of the most adored games of Nintendo’s catalog. The launch title of the Switch was The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, and was also the Wii U’s final game. The game was an open world exploration game where you could do anything in the order you wanted, still being one of the Switch’s best selling at the moment. As time has gone on, the Switch has released even more favorited games. Examples being Super Mario Odyssey, Kirby Star Allies, Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, Luigi’s Mansion 3, and Pokemon: Sword and Pokemon: Shield, being the fastest selling games in its lifetime. In just about 2 years the console has already sold 41 million copies. Since it is still technically a handheld, it is already starting to get different versions. The only one to come out so far is the Switch Lite, which is basically if the Switch was strictly a handheld and a bit smaller. Since its launch in September its sold around 2 million copies at the moment. As it is still technically new, it will probably be another 2 to 6 years before another console is released, and the Nintendo Switch will only keep growing.
